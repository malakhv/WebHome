Since 2014, my professional focus has been on Android (AOSP) and, later, Android Automotive (AAOS) – specifically, the development of system and end-user software for devices running these operating systems. Currently, I'm the head of the Yandex Auto product at Yandex – a solution built on Android Automotive OS that brings together Yandex services (such as Navigation, Alice, Music, Fuel, Parking, Weather, Video, and others) and makes them convenient to use in a vehicle. Beyond that, Yandex Auto is a complete HMI (Human-Machine Interface) solution that includes all the necessary components for managing the multimedia system (In-Vehicle Infotainment, IVI) and vehicle settings (HVAC, Body, etc.).

Beyond my day-to-day work, I have a strong interest in other areas of computer science, including compilers and programming languages, operating systems, databases, and embedded systems.
