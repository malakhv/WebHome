Since 2014, my professional interests related to Android (AOSP) and (later) Android Automotive (AAOS) — to the development of system and user software for devices on these operating systems. Right now, I'm head of Yandex Auto product in Yandex company — is a solution based on Android Automotive OS that combines Yandex services (such as Navigation, Alice, Music, Fuel, Parking, Weather, Kinopoisk, etc) and makes them convenient to use in a vehicle. Besides, Yandex Auto is a complete HMI solution (Human-Machine Interface) which contains all the necessary components for managing multimedia system (In-Vehicle Infotainment, IVI) and vehicle parameters (Vehicle Settings, HVAC, etc).

Also, my interests in computer science are compilers and programming languages, operating systems, databases, embedded systems.
