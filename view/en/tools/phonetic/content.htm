English Phonetic Alphabet

Combined with differing accents and bad phone lines, some letters and numbers can sound very similar when spoken, so how do you make sure they are not misinterpretted?

The best thing you can do is use a phonetic alphabet to spell out words letter by letter. There are a number of phonetic alphabets generated over time and for specialist purposes, but the most widely used is the one created by NATO.

This alphabet is also known as the International Radiotelephony Spelling Alphabet, the ICAO phonetic, the ICAO spelling alphabet or the ITU phonetic alphabet. Below you will find all the letters and numbers you need and also how to pronounce them properly.
