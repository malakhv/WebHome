My PC

  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 3GB by ASUS.
  • RAM: Kingston 4096 MB DDR3 PC3-12800 (KHX1600C9D3/4GX) x 4.
  • SSD 1: Kingston SV300S37A120G — 120 GB.
  • SSD 2: Kingston SA400S37480G — 480 GB.
  • SSD 3: Kingston SA400S37960G — 960 GB.
  • CD-ROM: ASUS BW-16D1HT.
  • Motherboard: ASUS H97-PRO
  • Monitor: Samsung SyncMaster 226bw (22-inch, 1680x1050, 60Hz).
  • Speakers: Microlab B37.
  • OS: Windows 7 Home Edition 64 bit, Service Pack 1 (on SSD 1).

As you can see, it's not exactly a modern computer, but it's enough for all my activities :)
